SORS

Semantic Observation for Remote Sensing

This web app is developed to make easier the analysis and the monitoring 
of areas at risk. Thanks to our knowledge base SORSOntology
it's possible to access to a lot of information about different zones and
it's also possible to help researchers by uploading their own images.
